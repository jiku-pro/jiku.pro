Jiku Core makes it easy.

Curated

Jiku Core consists of in-house and third-party software packages, all assembled into a common, consistent, and intuitive API, making it easy for you to plug-and-play different algorithms, routines, and procedures, tweak parameters, produce results quickly. Jiku Core streamlines your data processing tasks, frees your time, and lets your focus on your most important work: thinking and creating.

All packages are curated, developed and maintained by Jiku. We ensure that all tools do what they're supposed to do. We warn you if you use tools in unsupported scenarios. We have invested tens-of-thousands of people hours to develop and research this code, its capacities and and its limitations. Exploit our work and accelerate your own.

Our job is to make your job easier. Let us help.


Open

Jiku Core is fully open-source, licensed under GPLv3.

  • Non-commercial use?     100% free.
  • Open commercial use?     Yes! Provided your software (that surrounds, references or otherwise uses Jiku Core) is also released as free-and-open-source.
  • Closed commercial use?     Contact us.
